Mission Statement:

Miyu Yuuki is an employee of RE/MAX, a well respected real estate company known around the world.

I aim to be the most successful real estate agent in the Greater Tornoto Area (GTA). I have incorporated proven, professional state of-the-art techniques specializing in the marketing, listing and selling of new and resale luxury homes, residential communities, condominiums, home sites, undeveloped land and commercial and investment opportunities. I continually strive to provide top quality service for my clients and customers. I am a creative, innovative and people-oriented person who provides individual opportunity, personal satisfaction and rewarding challenges to all stakeholders I interact with.

Miyu's statement articulates core RE/MAX values and goals, while focusing on the shared mission to help others.

  • At RE/MAX, you’re in business for yourself, but not by yourself.

RE/MAX will always be the right place for real estate entrepreneurs who want a combination of independence, support, unique competitive advantages – and the right choice for customers who understand the benefits of having someone like that working on their behalf.
